<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5a026a9cffe75fbace00007f/train/java target="_blank">Circles in Polygons</a></h2><h3>8 kyu</h3><p>You own a box-making company that can produce boxes in the shape of any regular polygon.<br>Many customers use these boxes to transport circular objects - such as tin cans, glasses, tyres, or CDs.  </p><p>To assist them, you decided to print on every box the diameter of the largest circle that can fit inside the box.</p><h4 id="input">Input</h4><ul><li>The number of equal sides of the regular polygon (sides ≥ 3),</li><li>The length of each side of the polygon.</li></ul><h4 id="output">Output</h4><p>Return the diameter of the largest circular item that can fit inside the polygon, accurate up to 0.001.</p><h4 id="examples">Examples</h4><p>A box of 4 sides and a side length of 10 can hold a circular item of diameter 10.<br>A box of 8 sides and a side length of 9 can hold a circular item of diameter approx. 21.72792206.</p>
